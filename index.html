About me

I'm a normal middle-aged guy. Portuguese from Trás-os-Montes region, but born in Mozambique, in the beautiful city of Quelimane in the Zambézia district. Married and father of a beautiful teenager who is the light of my life. For three decades, I was a musician, which is now just a hobby. At the same time, for 20 years I was a Futsal coach, and now I am just a fan. I love to cook, play guitar, compose and produce music in my home studio. Some consider me a bookworm. I love the countryside, the sea, barbecue with friends... good wine, gin, caipirinha... At almost 50 years old, I decided to go back to school and get a degree..
